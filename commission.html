COMMISSION

Styles, What I do and Prices

Commissions are open!

When you order art from me, the width of themes and topics are almost eternal! Get a portrait of your loved ones, your pets, your house, yourself. Make me draw your original character for your novel and make your dream come real. Make me design a monster for your new game, make me design medieval, futuristic, or steampunk clothes for your tail. You can also buy my old drawings, if you can imagine one of them on your wall! 1

I also design decals, book covers, comic and manga characters, buildings2 and minimalistic 3D designs.

What I use for traditional drawing

Mechanical pencil, fineliner pens, black ink, brushpen, ProMarker pens, watercolour

What I use for digital drawing:

Wacom Intuos M, iPad Air 3, Adobe Photoshop, Procreate, Clip Studio Paint

What I use for 3D art:

3Ds Max, Zbrush, Quixel SUITE, Marmoset Toolbag

Disclaimer

1. My private artworks for sale can be found on: My Website, My Instagram and Etsy

2. Architectural drawings are strictly reduced to actual, existing streets, houses, interior spaces. If you need someone who dreams your imaginary house or tower, I suggest you get in contact with an interion designer

3. Although there are some topics that I consciously avoid (such as religion or atheism), an artist knows it’s strength and weaknesses. The lists of "What I do” or "What I do not” can change with time.

4. If you are asking for a commission, or buy an existing piece with my original characters on, remember, all of my original content is copyright. Please don’t steal my creatures, characters, ideas in your own work.

The deposit is £10 for ALL works. You will get this amount back when the artwork is ready and delivered. If you are not satisfied with the final piece, you will also get your deposit back, but I cannot pay the full fee back.

5. As with every freelance work, prices can have an extremely wide scale. A lot depends on the complexity of the drawing, how long it will take to finish, how much do I have to change after consulting. If it takes up a lot of time, difficult to draw and the deadline is soon, I might charge more in the end. Usually a simple, headshot lineart takes up 1 to 2 days – while a fully shadowed, backgrounded group picture can take up 7 to 9 days
