ABOUT ME

Illustrator, Writer and Amazing

I am Hanna Somos, 22, illustrator and writer. I suffer from panic disorder, and Borderline personality disorder. Being a junior illustrator, my goal is to break through the traditional limits of illustration, and leave all the compulsory trends behind.

By publishing my art my hope is to increase mental health awareness around the world. I never have certain plans of what I am going to draw, inspiration just hits me, then I sit down and draw until my hand gets a cramp. And there are no rules. Why would there be? Drawing is a way of expressing your deepest feelings, desires and fears: thatâ€™s why art therapy exists!

This is drawing for me: my sketchbook is my therapist, my diary, my priest, my best friend. I use my knowledge and my talent to create things that have never existed before, but were waiting to be born for ages. When you look at my art, you see me. Currently I am working on 4 novels, from which I would only like to publish one as an actual book, the others I would like to turn into a comic/cartoon series.

My final goal is to publish these stories to the world.
